Welcome to the Digital Clinical Safety Platform (DCSP). This DCSP is an application that is currently in the develpment stage. The app is being built by the Digital Clinical Safety Alliance.

The ultimate aim of the DCSP is to streamline and automate the process of clinical safety documentation of digital systems used in healthcare. It is also designed to engrain and also simplify the documentation and thought processes around improved clinical safety from the code up to upper management.

If you want to know more about the DCSP, or see a live demo, then please email [dcsp](mailto:dcsp.information@gmail.com).
